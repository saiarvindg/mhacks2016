myCart

An easy way to search and buy items
from your phone

  • Try it out

Send a text to (888) 888-8888

myCart will parse through the message to see if you want to buy an item from Wal-Mart's inventory. It will then search Wal-Mart's API to find exactly what you are looking for.

Returns item name and item price

Afterward, myCart will send you a text message displaying the item name, item price, and number of items left in inventory.

2-step confirmation process

To complete your order, you'll need to go onto our web application to confirm your orders. That's it!
